Welcome to!

WorkConnect

Empowering Informal Workers

WorkConnect is a mission-driven platform that empowers skilled informal workers by connecting them directly with households in need of essential services like plumbing, electrical work, and carpentry. In many urban areas, these workers remain invisible and underpaid due to a lack of digital presence. WorkConnect bridges this gap by offering a simple, tech-enabled booking solution that ensures fair wages, job transparency, and dignity of labor. Whether you're a homeowner seeking reliable help or an organization looking to support community livelihoods, WorkConnect makes it easy to get involved. Join us in building an inclusive economy—one job at a time.

Our Vision

WorkConnect

In just a short span, WorkConnect has demonstrated the real-world impact of connecting informal workers with dignified employment opportunities. Through our early pilot, we’ve successfully completed over 900+ service jobs, helping workers gain consistent income and households access reliable local services. We've provided digital literacy training to 12+ workers, enabling them to navigate online platforms and participate in the digital economy. With a 94% user satisfaction rate, our solution is not just functional—it’s meaningful and trusted. These early results validate our approach and push us forward in scaling our impact to hundreds more workers and communities.
